National Identity Card

National Identity Card (NID) is issued by the District Administration Office. Applicants must visit in person with original documents.

(राष्ट्रिय परिचय पत्र जिल्ला प्रशासन कार्यालयबाट प्रदान गरिन्छ)

1 What You’ll Need
i.
Citizenship Certificate

Original citizenship and photocopies too

ii.
Parent's Citizenship

Father or Mother citizenship copy

iii.
Online Application

Bring the softcopy of the filled online form

2 Where to Go

District Administration Office (DAO)

Office opens at 10 AM
View on Map

Online form

Apply Online

Must bring the softcopy of the filled online form

3 Average time

The average time is:- 1-2 Days

Token Required: Arrive before 9 AM for smooth processing.
Tip: Bring original documents and 2 photocopies for faster service.
