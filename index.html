Why We Built Civic Path

  • Many people visit the wrong office or miss documents
  • Information is often unclear or inconsistent
  • There is no idea how long services should normally take
  • This leads to wasted time, money, and frustration

Civic Path is a student-built initiative to make government services easier to understand, especially for first-time applicants and rural users.
